About Me

Hi. My name is Vasilis Smiris. I am a Computer Science graduate from Athens, Greece, currently looking to start a career as a software developer. Through university and personal projects, I have gained experience in various programming languages, software development frameworks and other technologies. Aside from technical knowledge, I have also gained a theoretical understanding of the principles of programming, of what makes a piece of software good and of how different technologies work and can be combined together to create complex applications. I consider myself a full-stack developer, being able to design and implement both the front-end and the back-end of an application and having an understanding of what external infrasructure, both in software and hardware, might be needed to make it deployable and usable. Through the years, I have mostly worked with the .NET Framework and the C# programming language, having built several desktop and web applications using the plethora of project types that the framework provides. My second most worked language is Python, which I greatly value for prototyping and experimenting with complex scientific subjects. Lately, I have been dabbling into other technological subjects that have gained my interest, like solving low-level problems using C++, game development and electronics. In my free time, I enjoy studying history and geography, getting informed about advancements in various scientific fields and infrasturcture projects, doing photo and video editing, cycling and traveling. Feel free to contact me.
