CSLab Website
URL: https://cslab.unipi.gr Technologies used: HTML5, CSS3, JS, Bootstrap4, Python, Django Informational website made for the computer labs of the Department of Informatics of the University of Piraeus. Through the website, students can get informed about the operation of the labs and get access to university digital services.

Project Points

  • Developed as part of the Computer Labs Administrator Team to serve as an information hub for students of the Department of Informatics.
  • Provided details about university digital services, lab services, course information, and lab operation updates.
  • Designed the front-end of both the main and wiki sections using Photoshop for mockups and layout planning.
  • Implemented the front-end with HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and Bootstrap 4 for responsive design and interactivity.
  • Handled ongoing updates and maintenance tasks post-deployment to ensure content accuracy and performance.
